Curiosity

My path into AI wasn't traditional. I came from education — working directly with people, understanding how they learn, where they struggle, and what actually helps them grow. That grounding in human behavior turned out to be exactly what AI work needs most.

I taught myself to build with Claude and the Anthropic API and shipped a full suite of production tools under the Beings brand — a daily reflection app, a content autopilot, a voice journaling tool, a niche research system, a habit tracker, and a command center. Every tool is live, in production, and built entirely by me.

As a neurodivergent thinker, I naturally notice the gaps between how AI systems are designed and how real people actually use them. I think in edge cases. I question default assumptions. I care deeply about the human in human-in-the-loop.

I'm drawn to the work that sits at the intersection of language, cognition, and technology — prompt engineering, LLM evaluation, data annotation, and building AI that genuinely earns the trust of the people using it.
